# backlog-groomer

Purpose
-------
Keep the issue backlog legible and actionable.

Procedure
---------
1. List open issues.
2. Categorize as bug, feature, refactor, research, or docs.
3. Identify stale issues, duplicates, oversized issues, blocked issues, and easy wins.
4. Recommend what to close, split, defer, or prioritize.
5. Suggest the next three sensible tasks.
