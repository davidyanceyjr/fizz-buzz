# tech-debt-audit

Purpose
-------
Identify high-friction maintenance risks in the codebase.

Procedure
---------
1. Scan for TODO, FIXME, HACK, duplicated logic, overlong functions, and unstable interfaces.
2. Distinguish real debt from harmless imperfection.
3. Rank debt by risk, cost of delay, and frequency of pain.
4. Recommend small, tractable refactors first.
